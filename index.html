@ Nordic CHI 2024
13th October 2024, Uppsala, Sweden
Email Position Paper

Abstract

The second edition of our workshop continues the conversation on social drones, focusing on their potential contributions to health, well-being, sports, and play. In this edition, our aim is to broaden the discussion by inviting a diverse group of researchers to delve into one of the prominent challenges in human-drone interaction: the acceptance of social drones in personal spaces. By expanding the workshop’s research focus, we intend to attract researchers from all relevant fields to provide their perspectives on this specific challenge. To stimulate discourse on this topic, the application focus of the drones will be on enhancing experiences related to health, well-being, sports, and play. Through a call for position papers, our goal is to convene a cohort of relevant researchers to collectively contribute to drafting a roadmap that identifies pertinent research avenues while considering various perspectives related to the use case. Ultimately, our objective is to address key challenges, establish research avenues to support a paradigm shift, and foster connections with researchers to promote the development of drones that enhance meaningful experiences, thus advancing the understanding and adoption of social drones in personal spaces. Paper format and submissions

Two-four pages position will be accepted. Paper should follow the standard single column ACM format, in accordance with the guidelines of the NordiCHI conference. For more information about the format, see the conference website: Nordic CHI 2024

The publication of a post-workshop journal special issue will be investigated and announced during the workshop

Submission Instructions

Position papers must be submitted electronically in PDF format to ( mobaid [at] chalmers [dot] se ) by the deadline. In addition to the position paper’s content, the submission should include a section at the end that outlines the authors’ interest and motivation around the workshop’s topic.
